Grilled Cheese

  • Time: appx. 7 min.
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Sharp cheddar cheese 4 thin slices
Tomato, ripe 3-4 thin slices
Bread of choice 2 slices
Mustard 1 tsp.
Salt & Pepper 1 pinch
Salted butter 1 tsp.

Directions: Spread butter onto outside of bread. Place first piece of bread, butter side down, into cold, non-stick skillet.
Spread mustard onto dry, upward-facing bread. Layer cheese and tomatoe, sprinkle salt and pepper onto tomato slices, and top with second slice of bread butter face out.
Cook over medium heat until golden brown on both sides. If bread browns before cheese melts, turn down the burner.

Creamed, Dried Beef

  • Time: appx. 20 min.
  • Serves: 3 to 4
Ingredients Quantity
Dried beef 1 12 oz. jar
Bread of choice 3-4 slices
Flour appx 3/4 C
Milk appx. 1/2 C
Ground Pepper appx. 1 tbsp.
Salted butter 1 cube (1/2 C)

Directions: Melt butter in medium skillet over low heat.
Fill dried beef jar with water and shake violently to remove as much salt as possible. This will prevent your gravy from being overly salty. Rinse and repeat several times. Dry with paper towel.
Over medium heat, slowly add flour using a whisk to prevent clumping.

Peanut Butter and Jelly

  • Time: appx. 2 min.
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Jelly 2-3 tsp.
Peanut Butter 2-3 tsp.
Bread of choice 2 slices

Directions: Spread peanut butter and jelly onto both pieces of bread and stack with dry side of bread on the outside of the sandwich.
